Ecommerce

It type of ecommerce solution that involves customer gathering goods, collecting information on the goods and receiving these products over an electronic network.
It online retail of electronics such as mobiles,laptops,tv,computers etc. It enables users to easily buy electronics over digital media.
User can select products from various categories and different vendors

Buy you went

Carts

Clicks on cart
Cart details along with subtotal for each product(quantity* productprice). Also displays grand total for all products with tax calculation.
User clicks on remove button to remove products from cart.
User clicks on proceed to checkout.

Checkout
